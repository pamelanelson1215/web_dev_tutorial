Biography

I was born in Boise, ID October 25th, 1999. When I was eleven years old, my family
moved to McCall, ID where we remained until I graduated McCall-Donnelly High
School in 2018. I grew up the oldest of four brothers. In 2011, my second to youngest
brother was diagnosed with cancer; he survived four years of treatment and is
currently in remission. This experience has changed my outlook on life and how
I live it.
In 2017, I applied and was accepted in Boise State Univeristy. Game, Interactive Media
and mobile technology was the major I chose and have stuck with it. My goal
is to graduate spring 2021.
